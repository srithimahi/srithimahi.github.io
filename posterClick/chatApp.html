art

drawing, painting, and making my brain calm down for five minutes

i’ve always bounced in and out of “art phases”, but this one actually stuck. now i draw a lot of girls with intense expressions, weird lighting, and the kind of color palettes that probably belong on album covers.

what i like making

  • character art with dramatic lighting and moody faces
  • paintings that look like stills from some nonexistent anime
  • sketchbook pages full of experiments that may or may not go anywhere

why it matters to me

art is the place where i can be messy on purpose. it also leaks into my tech stuff: ui design, color choices, little visual jokes on posters, and the way this whole site looks.

not everything here is “finished” or “portfolio-ready.” some of it is just me, trying things and seeing what feels right.
