tsa things

competitions, weird prompts, and that one trip to nashville

TSA is where school turned into “here’s a slightly unhinged challenge, go solve it with engineering and vibes.” it pushed me into events i wouldn’t have tried on my own and forced my brain into full problem-solving mode.

some highlights

  • competing in events that mixed engineering, creativity, and time pressure
  • working with teammates to pull projects together right before deadlines
  • going to nashville for nationals and living on caffeine + adrenaline

what i got out of it

  • how to break big problems into “we can actually finish this” chunks
  • how to present ideas in a way judges and normal humans understand
  • a lot of proof that i weirdly thrive under chaos

10/10 would stress over design docs and presentations again.
