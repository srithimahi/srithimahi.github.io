about this website

a poster wall, some chaos, and way too many tiny layout decisions

this site started because i didn’t want the Serious Developer Website™ with grayscale cards and bootstrap vibes. i wanted something a bit more chaotic.

what this site is for

this isn’t just “look at my projects”; it’s more like a little museum of things i’ve sunk time and braincells into: robotics, research, art, competitions, random ideas that somehow turned into real things.

what might happen later

  • wayyy more posters as i inevitably collect new projects
  • possibly a blog or devlog area if i decide to overshare even more

tl;dr: this website is my “here’s everything i care about, but make it cute” space.
