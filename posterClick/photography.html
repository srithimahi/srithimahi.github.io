photography

documenting light, colors, and the occasional unintentional cinematic moment

i’m not a super Serious Photographer™, but i like capturing little moments: weird lighting, empty streets, sky colors, and anything that looks like it could be a frame from a movie or anime opening.

what i like shooting

  • city lights at night and reflections on wet streets
  • clouds doing something dramatic for no reason
  • small details that most people walk past without noticing

how it connects to everything else

photography messes with my sense of composition and color in a good way. it sneaks back into how i frame ui layouts, choose color palettes, and even how i think about scenes in my art.

half my camera roll is “accidental” shots that ended up looking better than the ones i tried to pose.
