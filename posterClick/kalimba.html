kalimba

tiny metal tines, big feelings

the kalimba is my “what if music, but low-stress” instrument. no giant setup, no terrifying bow control, just a small wooden block that sounds way prettier than it has any right to.

why i like it

  • you can pick it up and play without a huge time commitment
  • it sounds soft and cozy, even when i miss notes
  • it’s perfect for late-night, brain-off music moments

what i play

mostly anime music, game soundtracks, and any melody that gets stuck in my head long enough that i have to exorcise it by plinking it out on the kalimba.

is it technically advanced? no. does it make my brain happy? absolutely.
