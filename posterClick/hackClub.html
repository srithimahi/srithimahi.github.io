hack club coppell

a club for people who think “what if we just built it ourselves?”

hack club coppell is our little corner of builders, coders, and people who show up saying “i have an idea, but i don’t know where to start” and leave with a half-working project and three new obsessions.

what we do

  • build random projects just because they sound fun or cursed
  • mess with web dev, hardware, ai, games, and whatever else people bring in
  • help each other debug things that broke five minutes before a demo

my role in all this

  • helping organize, brainstorm, and keep the chaos vaguely structured
  • supporting people who are new to coding but want to make something real
  • bringing my own projects in and letting people poke at them

it’s less “formal club” and more “group of gremlins who like building things and occasionally remember to take photos.”
